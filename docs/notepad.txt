calibration start: disable automations when we set fan temp to LOW, and afterwards re-enable them

zone ventilation:
on demand, we want the other zones to keep the same ventilation
as would be on low, but, we want extra ventilation on the demand zone.
So, when we set a fan to eg. high, and open up the valves for zone 1 (with the demand),
we want the other zones to close their valves to maintain the same ventilation as would be on low.
Or at least about the same.
